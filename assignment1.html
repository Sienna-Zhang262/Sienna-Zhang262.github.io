Staying Safe Online: Understanding Privacy, Security, and Media Ethics in the Digital Age How communication technology affects your data — and how you can protect yourself

Introduction

Every day, we use communication technologies—social media, messaging apps, school platforms—without thinking about what happens to the information we share. But behind every message, photo, or post, there are privacy risks and ethical responsibilities that affect both us and the people around us. Understanding these issues is the first step toward becoming a safer, more responsible digital citizen.
