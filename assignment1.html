Introduction

Every day, we use communication technologies—social media, messaging apps, school platforms—without thinking about what happens to the information we share. But behind every message, photo, or post, there are privacy risks and ethical responsibilities that affect both us and the people around us. Understanding these issues is the first step toward becoming a safer, more responsible digital citizen.In this blog post, I will explore two essential topics: 1，Privacy and Security Issues in Communication Technologies 2，Ethical Issues in Creating and Sharing Media I will also share personal examples and practical tips . By the end, you’ll have a clear understanding of how to navigate the digital world safely and ethically.
